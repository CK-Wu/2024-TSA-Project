Spiced Lentil Soup

What's in it?

Main: Lentils, onions, carrots, tomatoes.

Flavor: Garlic, ginger, and spices (turmeric, cumin).

Toppings: Cilantro, lemon, or yogurt (optional).

Farm-to-Table Connection

Lentils are sourced from regional growers focused on regenerative agriculture, promoting soil enrichment and water conservation. Aromatic spices like turmeric, cumin, and coriander can be purchased from fair-trade cooperatives, ensuring ethical sourcing. Seasonal root vegetables and herbs enhance the soup’s richness.

Preparation Processes

1. Rinse and soak lentils to reduce cooking time and improve digestibility.

2. Sauté onions, garlic, and spices to build a flavor base.

3. Simmer lentils with broth and vegetables until tender.
