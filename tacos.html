Jackfruit Tacos

What's in it?

Filling: Shredded jackfruit cooked with spices.

Toppings: Lettuce, salsa, guacamole, and lime crema.

Wrap: Soft tortillas.

Farm-to-Table Connection

Jackfruit, grown sustainably in tropical climates, is an excellent meat alternative that requires minimal resources to cultivate. Toppings like shredded lettuce, salsa, and guacamole highlight locally grown produce, emphasizing reduced transportation emissions and support for smaller-scale farms.

Preparation Processes

1. Shred canned or fresh jackfruit, then cook with spices to mimic a "pulled meat" texture.

2. Warm tortillas on a skillet for pliability.

3. Assemble with jackfruit, fresh toppings, and your choice of sauce.
