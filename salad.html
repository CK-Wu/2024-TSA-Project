Harvest Salad

What's in it?

Base: Mixed greens (kale, spinach, arugula).

Add-ons: Roasted root veggies, cherry tomatoes, apples, nuts/seeds.

Dressing: Tangy vinaigrette.

Farm-to-Table Connection

The Harvest Salad celebrates seasonal produce, directly sourced from local farms. Crisp greens, heirloom tomatoes, roasted root vegetables, and nuts or seeds reflect what's freshly harvested. By partnering with community-supported agriculture (CSA) programs, this salad supports sustainable farming practices and reduces food miles.

Preparation Processes

1. Wash and dry greens thoroughly to retain freshness.

2. Roast root vegetables with olive oil, salt, and herbs for depth of flavor.

3. Assemble ingredients just before serving to maintain crisp textures.
