Rainbow Wrap

What's in it?

Wrap: Whole-grain tortilla.

Filling: Colorful veggies (carrots, peppers, cabbage), beans or hummus, leafy greens, quinoa (optional).

Sauce: Tahini or avocado spread.

Farm-to-Table Connection

A colorful medley of fresh vegetables, beans, and grains wrapped in a whole-grain tortilla is the star of the Rainbow Wrap. Ingredients like bell peppers, spinach, red cabbage, and chickpeas are grown using organic methods, prioritizing biodiversity and soil health.

Preparation Processes

1. Prepare all vegetables by washing, chopping, and lightly seasoning.

2. Pre-cook grains or beans for filling.

3. Layer ingredients on the wrap, drizzle with a light dressing, and roll tightly.
